Random Cat Generator Favorites

What is this?

This web page will allow you to generate random images of cats...
Find pictures of cats as well as their information and add them to your favorites.
Certain pictures will have "Hairless" and when added to favorites will contribute to the hairless count




In the species box whatever species you type will be the species you get in return.

In the amount box type in the number of cat images you would like to generate

Once you've generated your images you can remove or add it to your favorites. If you would like to remove all images click CAT again.
